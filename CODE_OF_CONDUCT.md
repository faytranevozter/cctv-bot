# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making CCTV Bot a respectful and harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, identity, or perspective.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for the impact of your words and actions.
- Keep feedback focused on the code, documentation, security, and project goals.
- Be careful with operational details. Do not publish private camera URLs, credentials, Telegram bot tokens, chat IDs, database files, or logs containing sensitive data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption of project discussions.
- Publishing another person's private information without explicit permission.
- Posting secrets, production credentials, private stream URLs, or exploit details in public project spaces.
- Encouraging unsafe usage that bypasses authorization or exposes cameras without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently limit participation in project spaces.

Enforcement actions will be based on the severity and frequency of the behavior and the risk to contributors or users.

Reports will be reviewed as privately as practical. Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern or participates in an investigation is not acceptable.
